About

I work on problems in mechanism design, with a focus on multidimensional screening in a variety of economic settings.

I hold a PhD in Economics from the Indian Statistical Institute, Delhi, where I worked under the supervision of Prof. Debasis Mishra .

I am currently reading and learning optimal transport, with an interest in its connections to mechanism design.
